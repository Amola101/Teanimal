European Coalition to End Animal Experiments

The European Coalition to End Animal Experiments are working hard to help eliminate all animal testing within the European Union. They’ve ended animal testing for cosmetics in the EU and have delivered globally recognized cruelty-free standards for cosmetics, the Humane Cosmetics Standard.

PETA

PETA’s page is about fighting against regulatory testing on animals and the resources they’re using to fight it. They’ve donated more than one million dollars to find non-animal testing methods. They’ve proposed many solutions for the United States to shift away the use of animal testing products.

Coalition for Consumer Information on Cosmetics

The Coalition for Consumer Information on Cosmetics has a Leaping Bunny program to let consumers know what products are and are not cruelty-free. This means when you’re wandering through the store, you can shop with confidence and do a quick check to compare which brands are cruelty-free.
